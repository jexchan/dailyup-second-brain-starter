# Critical Check Examples

Use these only to clarify a request that matches the scenario.

## Knowledge Card

**User:** `质疑一下这张卡片：[[insight_学习不是输入而是重组]]`

**Focus:** Read the card, map its claims and support, then identify assumptions, counterexamples, boundaries, and a safer rewrite.

## Product or Learning Claim

**User:** `这个观点站得住吗？AI 会让学习变成一种即时反馈系统。`

**Focus:** Separate factual and methodological claims; inspect causal links, applicability boundaries, and evidence required.

## Draft Red Team

**User:** `帮我 red team 一下这篇文章草稿。`

**Focus:** Restate the central argument, then examine evidence, reasoning, counterexamples, and expression.

## Course Assumptions

**User:** `帮我校验这个课程设计里的假设。`

**Focus:** Identify assumptions about learners, objectives, practice, and assessment; prioritize the assumptions that require validation.
